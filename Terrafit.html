Photo Manipulation | Visual Identity

Terrafit

The "Terrafit" project is a captivating photo manipulation initiative designed to convey the brand's global appeal. With no access to international models, we strategically utilized creative techniques to create visuals that suggest Terrafit’s presence on the international stage. Through skillful digital manipulation, we incorporated elements that resonate with a western aesthetic, showcasing the brand’s quality and style in a way that speaks to a diverse global audience.

    Proudly a homegrown Filipino brand, Terrafit is driven by the vision to be recognized globally. Through our designs, we aim to prove that Filipino-made footwear can stand proudly alongside the world’s finest, showcasing the best of both style and skill from the Philippines.
