Kopi Daily

The "Kopi Daily" project centers on crafting a simple, approachable design that instantly connects with its target audience. Featuring a clever combination of coffee, smoke, and a customized font, the design perfectly captures the shop's warm and inviting atmosphere. This unique blend enhances the brand's identity, making Kopi Daily not just a beverage stop, but a memorable and welcoming experience for all who visit.
